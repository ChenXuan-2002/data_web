    Abstract: HIV self-testing (HIVST) has been shown to complement existing facility-based testing services and promote regular HIV testing among men who have sex with men (MSM). Factors identified in the regression analyses were subsequently included in a structural equation modeling framework. A total of 178 individuals (35.7%) reported HIVST in the last 6 months. In the IMB model, information (Î² = 0.118, p = 0.027), motivation (Î² = 0.263, p < 0.001), and behavioral skills (Î² = 0.149, p = 0.002) each demonstrated a direct positive effect on HIVST.
